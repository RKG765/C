Relational Opertors in C

Relational Opertors are used to evaluate conditions (true or false) inside the if statement.

'>=' means greater than or equal to.
'>' means greater than.

NOTE:'=' is used for assignment where as '==' is used for equality cheak.
The conditions can be any vald expression.In C a non-zero value is considered to be true.
