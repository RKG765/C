Why Loops

Sometimes we want our program to execute few sets of instructions again and again.For example printing 1 to 100, first 100 even numbers.
Hence Loops make it easy for a programmer to tell computer that a given set of instructions must be executed.

The Break-Statement

The break-statement is used to exit the loop irrespective of whether the condition is true or false.
Whenever a "break" is encountered inside the loop, the control is sent outside the loop.
It can be used in for , while, do-while loops.

Continue Statement

Continue Statement is used to immideately move to the next iteration of the loop.
The control is taken to the next iteration thus skipping everything below "continue" inside the loop for that iteration.
