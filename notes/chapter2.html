Operator Precedence in C

3*x -8y is (3x)-(8y) or 3(x-8y)?
In C language simple mathematical rules like BODMAS, no longer applies.
The answer to the above question is provided by Operator Precedence & Associativity.

Operator Precedence:Priority

The following table lists the opertaors priority in C.
Priority------Operators
1st ------------ *, /, %
2nd ------------ +, -
3rd ------------ =
Operator of higher priority are evaluated first in the abesence of parenthesis.

Operator Associativity

When operators of equal priority are present in an expression, the tie is taken care of by associativity.Like:
x*y/z ---- (x*y)/y
x/y*z ---- (x/y)*z.
*,/ follows left to right associativity.
